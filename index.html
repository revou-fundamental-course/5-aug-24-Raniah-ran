Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement that compares a person's weight and height to assess whether they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Jenis Kelamin






    Advantages of BMI include:
  1. Simple and Easy to Calculate: BMI only requires height and weight, making it an easy and quick measure that can be calculated without specialized equipment.
  2. Cost-Effective: Since BMI relies on basic measurements, it is a cost-effective method for assessing weight status in large populations or individuals.
  3. Widely Used and Standardized: BMI is a globally recognized measure with standardized categories, which helps in comparing health data across different regions and populations.
  4. Good for Population-Level Screening: It is effective in identifying trends in obesity and underweight conditions across populations and is commonly used for public health assessments.
  5. Predictive of Health Risks: BMI can indicate potential health risks associated with being overweight or underweight, such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and certain cancers.

Although BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not measure body fat directly, and other factors like muscle mass, bone density, and distribution of fat that should be considered too <3
